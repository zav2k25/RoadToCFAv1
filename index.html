About

I'm a penultimate-year Economics student and CFA Level I candidate publishing equity research alongside my exam prep. Each research note starts with a public filing or market catalyst and works through a structured framework: industry context, financial model, and a clear investment thesis. The CFA curriculum informs how I read financial statements and think about valuation — the research is where I apply it.
